Timeline video


The timeline focuses on promoting albums

Part 1 lists your followed artists and plots them based on how you've rated their albums and the last time they've released one. 

The volume filter compares your rating position to users of different volumes while the genre tab shows how those artists are rated in different genre communities. 

The top albums toggle lets you compare the top 5 albums of a year to your 5 highest rated albums and you can add some friends.

Most anticipated toggle promotes upcoming albums. 




Second part of the timeline showcases the top albums of the month.

Clicking on an artist in your timeline will list that artist's albums and how you've rated each. 

Clicking on an album will show that album's rating in different genre communities and you can further filter by user volume





Part C of the timeline is a calendar where each slot is taken by an album, allowing users to compare their highest rated album of that month, year or half-decade. 

Users who rate the most albums will have a higher ranking on this calendar. 

The Top album toggle shows the highest rated album for each month and reveals a ranking on the top right of each album to show their distance to the top album. 

The volume button filters the users by their volumes and the genre button specifies users in a genre community, so you can see the top albums there.

You can also add some friends and compare your favorite albums.
