Online Multi-user Discovery and Promotion App

Quick Description

An online multi-user discovery and promotion app

Users select artists on a selection screen.

Based on the selections, users are grouped into rooms with users that share similar tastes

In each room, users swipe through 30-second audio previews from Spotify, added by other room members.

Discover new songs by swiping left or right, and add your own selections to the queue.

Interact with other users by selecting artists from a shared pool.

Track how your recommendations perform with other users in real-time.

Experience a dynamic environment as users come and go, influencing the room's musical direction.

Muffle is all about music discovery and promotion
